FAQ (frequently asked questions)


Do you have gift certificates?

Gift certificates are available for purchase in the salon or over the phone. If you purchase a grift certificate over the phone, we can either mail it to your lucky receipient or simply keep it in our files. Friseur Salon Patrice gift certificates are not redeemable for cash or refundable.

Can I bring my pet along?

Yes, we love pets! Please just make sure they're clean.

What awards did you receive so far?

Awards are my passion! I received quite a few after the years:
  • French Hairdressing Award 2014 (1st place)
  • Austrian Hairdressing Award 2015 (1st place)
  • HJ's 2016 British Hairdressing Award (2nd place)
